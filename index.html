About Us

Who We Are

The National Association of Retirees of State Government of Nigeria (NARSGON) is a body established to represent, protect and promote the interests of retired employees of state governments throughout Nigeria.

The association fosters unity, advocates for improved pension administration, supports welfare initiatives and promotes the well-being of retirees nationwide.

Our Vision

To be the foremost voice and support network for retirees, ensuring dignity, security and improved well-being for all retired state government workers in Nigeria.

Our Mission

To advocate for retirees, promote unity, engage stakeholders on pension matters and provide support that improves the lives of retired state government employees.
